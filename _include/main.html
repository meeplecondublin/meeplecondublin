About the Con

Meeple Con is a day of board gaming, focused on longer and/or high complexity games (examples include Splotter Games, Pax Games, Through the Ages, Mage Knight, Eclipse, etc). These games often are hard to get to the table due to length or rules complexity. That said we are open to any games people want to facilitate and people want to play. To help create the best experience we are looking for people to volunteer to facilitate their game, and the con will organize getting the players needed. This ensures every one is playing a game, players can learn the rules in advance and games can be sized for best the experience.

Con Director: Aidan Duggan
